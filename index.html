Escape Route™ is satire. It is the anti-map — where privacy tools route you around surveillance, this routes you directly through all of it, using real camera locations from OpenStreetMap (the same open data the DeFlock project maps) and road routing by OSRM. Soundtrack and co-driver are generated in your browser. Please do not use this to actually escape anything. You will be seen, logged, scored, and gently waved at. Camera data is community-sourced and incomplete — absence of a camera here is not absence of a camera there.
